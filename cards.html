Heuristic Evaluation of Virginia's EZ pass system's official website

Project at RIT

February 2017 - May 2017

The Aim of the project was to find a system and do a heuristic evaluation of the website. All of us first discussed to see which was the website are we going to study and after a lot of discussion, we decided to study, the official website for Virginia’s EZ Pass. We all first performed heuristic evaluations individually to identify the usability and accessibility issues in the website. Once this was done, we all sat and discussed each of our heuristic evaluations and came up with a document which was a compilation of all of the problems we found with the website. A total of 27 usability and accessibility issues was found with the website.
