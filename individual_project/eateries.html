Eateries

1. Vidhyarthi Bhavan

This place was opened in 1943 and is set to have the best Masala dosas in the city. It is always crowded, and you cannot ever get in there without waiting. Their Masala dosas are very unique and the restaurant serves a total of only 3 or 4 dishes and coffee. Bangalore is know for its Idlis and dosas (South Indian food).

2. Brahmin's Coffee Bar

This place was opened in the 1960s and is set to have the best Idli, Vada Chutni in the city. Their Chutney is very unique and very tasty. The place is also known for good coffee.

3. Hotel Nandhini

This hotel was set up in 1989 and is known famously for its Andhra cuisine/ Andhra meals. The food is very unique, spicy and delicious.

4. Sri Venkateshwara Sweet Meat stall

This was initially started in 1954 and then expanded. It is very well known for its chaats like pani puri, dahi puri etc. It is also very well known for its sweets.

5. Shiv Sagar

This place came into being in the 1990s. It is very well known for its South and North Indian food. I personally love the gobi manchurian and paneer butter masala the best.

6. Food Street

This is a street filled with stalls of food on the whole street and has South Indian cuisine, chaats, street food etc. The food here is just amazing and this street is so crowded on weekends, that you won't get a chance to step a foot inside.

7. Mainland China

This is an Indo-Chinese restaurant which is very famous for its Indo-Chinese food and has a lot of vegetarian options too.

8. Adyar Ananda Bhavan

This is a South Indian restaurant whose origin is from Tamil Nadu. It's very famous for its Sambar and Vada and many other dishes.

9. UB City

This is a very high-class mall and has an open setting on its roof with a lot of high-end restaurants and bars (all kinds of cuisines) and live music.

10. Punjab Grill

This is a very nice punjabi restaurant with spicy and delicious Punjabi dishes. They have added a lot of innovation into some of their dishes.

11. Barbeque Nation

This is a classy restaurant which serves barbeque and a lot of other dishes along with a north Indian buffet. They have a wide variety of innovative appetizers and there is a wide variety even for vegetarians.

12. Anand Sweets

This place is very well known for its sweets and chaats. I love the pani puri the best over here.
