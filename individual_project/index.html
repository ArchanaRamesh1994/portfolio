About Bangalore, India

The Garden city of India

Bangalore, also known as the Silicon Valley or IT Capital of India, is the capital of the state of Karnataka. It is also called the Garden city of India as it has numerous gardens, parks, monuments, temples etc. Some of the famous parks are LalBagh and Cubbon Park. The city has a rich heritage and also has grown exponentially in the last 10 years with the influx of the IT industries. There are many places to visit and the the variety of cuisines offered in the city are overwhelming. Blessed with the pleasant weather conditions throughout the year, people are bound to have an enjoyable holiday, irrespective of the time of the year they visit Bangalore.
