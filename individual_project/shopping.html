Shopping

1. Nalli Silks

This is a very big store known for its beautiful sarees. Its famous for its Kancheepuram sarees. It was founded in 1928 in Chennai and has expanded since then.

2. Commertial Street

This street is known to have a lot of shops and the prices are pretty reasonable.

3. Bhavani Kangan Store

This store is very famous for its jewellery (bangles, necklaces, earrings, etc.)

4. Orion Mall

This is a very huge mall and has lots of brands to shop for both western and traditional clothing.

5. GRT

GRT is a very famous store known for its gold ornaments. People go to buy traditional gold necklaces, bangles, earrings etc.

6. Forum Mall

This is a very huge mall and has lots of brands to shop for both western and traditional clothing.
